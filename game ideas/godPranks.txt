Your character is walking down the street towards his destination. Various objects will come in the way that can kill him. You need to set those objects in motion so that they try to kill him. He will dodge them in the beginning but his distress level will increase. Every time you fail to use any object, his distress level will decrease. If his distress level is full, he wont be able to dodge the next attack. So, your goal is to kill the man before he reaches his destination
